Dungeons and Dragons

This goes along with the games I like but it resides in its own category.

I love D&D and I am both a player and a DM (Dungeon Master). Being a dungeon master has its perks as you are the one who provides a world for your players to explore and fight monsters and whatever else you want.

I am currently running an online campaign and am looking to start an in-person campaign soon. Below will share details of the in-person campaign.

Campaign: Curse of Strahd

Curse of Strahd is a campaign that takes place in the land of Barovia. It is a land that is cursed by the vampire Strahd von Zarovich. The players will be tasked with stopping Strahd from taking over the land and killing the people of Barovia.

Player count: 0/5

Currently I am looking for 5 players to join my campaign. I am looking for players who are willing to commit to the campaign and are willing to play at least once a week.
